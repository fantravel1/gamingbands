    Frequently Asked Questions About the Band Builder

    How do I create my own virtual band?

    Use GamingBands.com's free Band Builder tool. Simply: 1) Enter a band name, 2) Choose a genre from 12 options, 3) Pick a game universe setting, 4) Select a stage aesthetic, 5) Define your narrative arc, then click Generate. You'll receive a complete band profile with lore, members, setlist, and a shareable link.

    Is the Band Builder tool free to use?

    Yes, the Band Builder on GamingBands.com is completely free. You can create unlimited band profiles, generate shareable links, and build as many virtual bands as you want with no account required.

    Can I share my virtual band with friends?

    Absolutely! After generating your band, click the "Copy Share Link" button to copy a unique URL. When anyone opens that link, they'll see your complete band profile with the same name, genre, universe, aesthetic, narrative, lore, members, and setlist.

    What genres can I choose for my virtual band?

    The Band Builder offers 12 unique genres: Chromatic Rock, Heavy Metal, Synth-Pop, J-Pop/Idol, Hip-Hop, Medieval Folk, Space Ambient, Electronic/EDM, Punk, Classical/Orchestral, Lo-Fi Chill, and Dark Wave. Each genre generates unique themed member names, setlist track names, and lore.
